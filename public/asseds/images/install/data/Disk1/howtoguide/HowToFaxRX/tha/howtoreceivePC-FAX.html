PC-FAX receiving

Viewing new PC-FAX messages
  1. Click Receive.
  2. Follow the on-screen instructions.
  3. The PC-FAX icon appears in your computer’s tasktray.
  4. Double-click the PC-FAX icon.
Note
If you want to return your machine to the default fax receive mode, change PC-FAX Receive to OFF from the Fax settings by pressing Menu or on your machine.
Faxes received into your computer will be saved in the following folder.
Windows XP; C:\Documents and Settings\All Users\Documents\BrFaxRx\PC-FAX Rx
Windows Vista or later; C:\Users\Public\Documents\BrFaxRx\PC-FAX Rx
